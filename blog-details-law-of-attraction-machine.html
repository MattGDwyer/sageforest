The Law of Attraction Machine

  • Casey
  • Sep 7, 2022

It is rather the case that we desire something because we believe it to be good, but that we believe a thing to be good because we desire it. It is the thought that starts things off.
        â€• Aristotle

The father of metaphysics said it first, it is the thought that begins everything.

If we want something, it is the belief that it will bring more joy, happiness and excitement to our lives. This can include anything, money, love, a better job or even children. Our feelings behind the things we want are the key.

I teach and follow the law of attraction, and according to this law, what you feel comes to you. Think of it as a machine that calculates what you feel and generates exactly that for you. It does not distinguish between good and bad, it only brings you what energy you give.

This is where many people struggle, and have unwanted experiences in their lives.

For example, if you wanted more money and were frustrated with the lack of it, guess what the law of the attraction machine will continue to generate for you? The lack of money.

If someone is struggling with their love life and finding the right partner, and their current reality leads them to believe that dating is difficult, monogamy is lost, and there are no good women / men left, guess again what the generator will bring them? Yep, more of the same.

So how can we get this law of attraction machine to compute what we want? It all is in how we feel. The feeling comes first. If you focus on the lack of something, it will bring you more of that lack. Instead, if you start feeling good about the thought of more money coming in, that is a good start. Imagine positive feelings around money, the ease it brings in, and the freedom you would feel. Start broad, then get more specific as you get better at it. The key is feeling good.

If you have trouble in love, imagine the kind of person you want to be with. Feel good with the thoughts of cuddles and Netflix, spending time with them and enjoying each other. Feel that there are so many people out there that want the same. Feel excited about the future. There are many feelings in this paragraph, but it is the key!

The machine will bring you whatever you are feeling.

So the best way to start is to just feel good. I understand that some of our realities make that difficult, but begin by looking at things that would make you feel good. Use your imagination. Just those steps alone can go a long way to begin changing the momentum of attraction.
