About

Casey Leonard

Welcome to Sage Forest!

I'm Casey Leonard, the face behind Sage Forest.

Come here for metaphysical content mixed with science backed resources. I hold a bachelor's degree in Psychology and a master's degree in Metaphysics. Through my personal growth and education, I have found my spiritual path and shared ways to live a life in which we have control over our reality. Using the Law of Attraction, we can live our own creation of what makes us uniquely happy, no matter what happens in the world or how others live around us.

Traditional talk therapy is a helpful way to navigate issues, recognize trauma, and limiting beliefs that are holding you back. Metaphysical tools, such as trauma release meditation, allow us to energetically and safely release unconscious trauma hidden within our psyche. Did you know that long-held negative emotions can cause scars within our bodies by blocking our energy and causing physical illness? By recognizing changes that need to be made in our lives and becoming more in harmony with ourselves, you can live your life in a way that frees up your energy to flow freely, allowing health and more success.
